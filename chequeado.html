Developing

Anti disinformation

We achieved what we could only dream of: convene over 120 media from all around the country, diverse in both size and editorial point of view, to work together against disinformation during the electoral campaign. The result? Reverso, the first and most ambitious collaborative journalistic project in Argentina.

Community

We gave our supporters a name of their own. The Chequeadistas are fact-checking activists committed to a more pluralistic and transparent debate. And we want to be more. Join us!

Innovation

We strengthened the Chequeabot. Our robot incorporated new features that facilitate journalistic work during live fact-checks and allow us to do our verification work faster without compromising quality.

Education

We achieved a record number of trainings in 11 provinces. We trained more than 450 journalists from all over the country on how to debunk misinformation and we we gave online courses and workshops for the whole region. And we also created Medio Flash, a media experiment to educate teens in critical thinking through entertainment.

Transparency

Because we advocate access to information and openness of data, we want you to be able to check on us. That’s why our funds and funding sources are public, as is our methodology.

Team

We love to create and experiment, we are dynamic and diverse; we share a common goal: to improve public debate, o develop antidotes to misinformation and to raise the cost of lying.
